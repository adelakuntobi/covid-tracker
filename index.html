CORONA VIRUS DISEASE (COVID-19)

Stay home! Mask up! stay safe!

The CoronaVirus Disease (Covid-19) is a contagious disease caused by CoronaVirus. The disease started in 2019 with its first case in China, and has since then, spread across various countries across the world. Currently, a mode of treatment has not been confirmed for this disease. It is why the necessary measures should be taken to prevent infection. As the popular saying goes; “Prevention is better than cure.”

How does covid spread?

The coronavirus disease can be spread in three major ways:

Contaminated Objects & substances.

Respiratory secretions or droplets
expelled by infected individuals
can contaminate objects and surfaces.

Air Transmission of Droplets and secretions

Infected droplets are spread through the
air when an infected individual coughs or sneezes.

Transmission Among Humans

Can occur during direct, indirect
or close contact with infected people
through infected secretions like saliva.

Symptoms Of Coronavirus

COVID-19 affects people in different ways. Majority of infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and get better without being hospitalized.

The incubation period for the virus ranges from 6-14 days. Recovery from covid symptoms takes about to weeks for people with mild cases and ranges form 3-6 week for people with serious cases.

As stated by the World Health Organisation (WHO), the following are symptoms of Covid-19.

  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Excess Sneezing
  • Aches and Pains
  • Diarrhoea
  • Inability to taste or smell.
  • Chest pains
  • Inability to move or speak
  • Breathing Difficulty
